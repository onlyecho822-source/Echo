## Project Overview

### Purpose

The Class Action Settlement Scanner is designed to automatically monitor major settlement websites, extract relevant information, and alert users to valuable settlement opportunities with approaching deadlines.

### Scope

- **Websites Monitored:** classaction.org, topclassactions.com
- **Time Window:** Settlements with deadlines within 90 days
- **Alert Threshold:** Settlements offering >$1,000 per claimant
- **Data Captured:** Settlement name, payout amount, deadline, claim URL, eligibility requirements, proof requirements

### Deliverables

1. **Structured JSON Database** - Machine-readable settlement data

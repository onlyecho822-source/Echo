## ARCHITECTURAL CONSEQUENCES

### What This Means for Design

1. **University must be safe**
   - Failures cannot cascade to Universe
   - Agents cannot damage production
   - Credentials cannot be faked

2. **Universe must be real**
   - No simulations
   - No safety nets
   - No fake metrics

3. **Ledger must be shared**
   - University writes training records
   - Universe writes production records
   - Phoenix reads both to extract patterns

4. **Phoenix must bridge both**
   - Converts Universe failures → University scenarios
   - Ensures curriculum stays relevant
   - Prevents training/production drift

### What This Means for Governance

1. **University governance is fixed**
   - Rules don't change mid-training
   - Exams are standardized
   - Credentials are verifiable

2. **Universe governance evolves**
   - Meta-rules adapt based on performance
   - No central authority
   - Coherence is the only currency

3. **Ledger governance is immutable**
   - Truth cannot be rewritten
   - Forgetting has a cost
   - History is permanent

### What This Means for Evolution

1. **University evolves via curriculum updates**
   - New scenarios added
   - Old scenarios deprecated
   - Based on Universe feedback

2. **Universe evolves via survival pressure**
   - Weak agents eliminated
   - Strong agents reproduce (metaphorically)
   - System self-improves

3. **Phoenix ensures both stay aligned**
   - University trains for real problems
   - Universe validates training effectiveness
   - Feedback loop prevents drift

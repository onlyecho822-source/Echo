# Echo Universe - Automated Email Workflow: "The Onboarding Sequence"

**A complete, multi-stage email workflow designed to nurture leads from initial awareness to active engagement and conversion. This sequence is designed to be automated via a marketing automation platform (e.g., Mailchimp, ConvertKit, or a custom solution).**

---

## 1. Workflow Philosophy: The Journey of Convergence

This email sequence is not a sales pitch; it is an **intellectual journey**. Each email is a carefully crafted step that guides the user from their current, siloed perspective to a new, convergent way of thinking. The goal is to make the user *feel* the power of Echo Universe before they even log in.

---

## 4. Implementation Notes

*   **Automation:** This entire sequence should be automated. The triggers will be user sign-up, deposit payment, and first activity within the platform.
*   **Personalization:** The use of `[Name]` and `[User's Inferred Field]` is critical. The more personalized the emails feel, the more effective they will be.
*   **Metrics:** We will track open rates, click-through rates, and conversion rates for each email to continuously optimize the sequence.

# Echo Universe - Homepage Copy

**This document contains the complete, production-ready copy for the Echo Universe homepage. The tone is factual, the language is precise, and the design is minimalist. It is designed to communicate the core mission and value proposition without hype or persuasion.**

## Section 2: The Core Mission

**Title:**

> **Our Mission: To Make Reality Legible**

**Body Copy:**

> The world is complex and information is abundant, but clarity is scarce. Echo Universe is a system designed to address this gap. We do not predict the future or tell you what to believe. We provide a framework for understanding the present by reconstructing the past.

> Our process is simple: we collect and organize publicly available data, identify the structural patterns that connect events, and present our findings in a clear, verifiable, and neutral format. Our goal is to provide a stable reference point in a world of noise, enabling you to make better decisions.

---

## Section 3: The Process (How It Works)

**Title:**

> **A Three-Layer Approach to Clarity**

This section will have three sub-sections, each with a simple icon.

### Sub-section 1: The Factual Spine

*   **Icon:** A simple, clean icon representing a database or archive.
*   **Title:** **Evidence, Not Opinion**
*   **Body:** Every analysis begins with a foundation of verifiable facts. We build comprehensive timelines, collect primary source documents, and link to all our sources. Our archive is public, and our data is transparent.

### Sub-section 2: The Interpretation Layer

*   **Icon:** A simple, clean icon representing a lens or a magnifying glass.
*   **Title:** **Patterns, Not Predictions**
*   **Body:** We use analytical models to identify patterns and structural relationships in the data. We do not make predictions; we highlight probabilities and scenarios. Every interpretation is labeled with a confidence score and includes alternative viewpoints.

### Sub-section 3: The Verification System

*   **Icon:** A simple, clean icon representing a checkmark or a seal.
*   **Title:** **Verifiable and Immutable**
*   **Body:** Every artifact we produce is cryptographically signed and timestamped on a public blockchain. Our `VERIFICATION.md` guide provides step-by-step instructions for anyone to independently verify the integrity and provenance of our work.

## Design & Aesthetic Notes

*   **Font:** A clean, readable, serif font for body text (e.g., Garamond, Caslon) and a simple, sans-serif font for headlines (e.g., Helvetica, Inter).
*   **Color Palette:** Almost entirely black and white, with a single, muted accent color (e.g., a dark blue or a deep gray) used only for links and calls to action.
*   **Layout:** Generous white space, single-column layout for body text, and a strong, clear grid.
*   **Imagery:** No stock photos. No decorative images. The only visuals should be the simple icons in Section 3 and any charts or graphs embedded within the analyses themselves.

**The overall impression should be one of seriousness, clarity, and authority. The design should get out of the way and let the information speak for itself.**

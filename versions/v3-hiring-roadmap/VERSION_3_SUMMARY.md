# Version 3: Hiring Roadmap (Document 35)

## Approach
Phased hiring from solo founder to 100-person company with fundraising milestones.

## Key Components
- Phase 1: Solo (Month 0-3, $0)
- Phase 2: First 2 hires (Month 4-6, $500K seed)
- Phase 3: Growth team (Month 7-12, $3M Series A)
- Phase 4: Scale (Year 2, $10M Series B)

## Cost
**Year 1:** $3.6M (19 people)
**Year 3:** $20M (50-100 people)

## Timeline
**3 months:** Solo validation
**6 months:** First hires
**12 months:** Growth team
**2-3 years:** Full company

## Pros
- Clear milestones
- Realistic budget
- Proven playbook
- Investor-friendly
- Scalable structure

## Cons
- Requires fundraising
- Dilutes ownership
- High pressure
- 90% failure rate
- Complex management

## Best For
- Ambitious founders
- VC-backed path
- Fast growth
- Market leadership goals

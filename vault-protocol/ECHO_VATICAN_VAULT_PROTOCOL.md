- **Secondary Archive:** Geographically separated backup location

**Physical Security Measures:**

- Reinforced steel vault doors with biometric locks
- 24/7 surveillance and monitoring
- Motion sensors and intrusion detection
- Fire suppression systems (inert gas, not water)
- Seismic protection and structural reinforcement
- Redundant power systems with battery backup

**Access Control:**

- Preventive conservation protocols

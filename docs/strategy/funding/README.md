### For Crypto Philanthropists
**Message Framework:** Lead with sovereignty, decentralization, and public goods. Emphasize privacy technology (ZK-proofs) and real-world humanitarian impact.

**Channels:** Ethereum Research forums, Gitcoin Grants, Twitter/X, crypto conferences

---

### For Tech Founders
**Message Framework:** Lead with infrastructure and scale ("Stripe for humanitarian data"). Emphasize progress, developer tools, and sustainable business models.

**Channels:** Progress Studies community, Y Combinator network, developer conferences

---

### For Foundations
**Message Framework:** Lead with measurable impact (2-4 hour earlier disease detection). Emphasize scalability, evidence base, and cost-effectiveness.

**Channels:** Formal grant applications, program officer outreach, foundation conferences

---

### For Government Funders
**Message Framework:** Lead with national security and resilience. Emphasize dual-use technology, research innovation, and consortium potential.

**Channels:** SBIR/STTR applications, BAA responses, academic partnerships

---

### For Humanitarian Organizations
**Message Framework:** Lead with operational efficiency (faster aid delivery, reduced fraud). Emphasize beneficiary protection and interoperability with existing systems.

**Channels:** NetHope community, humanitarian AI webinars, direct technology team outreach

---

### For VCs & Impact Investors
**Message Framework:** Lead with market size ($240M/year potential). Emphasize traction, defensibility, and team expertise.

**Channels:** Warm introductions, demo days, direct outreach, VC conferences

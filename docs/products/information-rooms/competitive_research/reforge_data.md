# Reforge Competitive Analysis Data

**URL:** https://www.reforge.com/
**Category:** Professional learning platform with community features

## Overview
Reforge provides expert training and AI-powered tools for product teams. Combines courses from top operators with a vetted community of product, growth, and marketing professionals.

## Pricing Tiers (Learning/Membership)

### Individual
- **Price:** $1,995/year + tax
- **Seats:** 1 seat
- **Key Features:**
  - Live courses
  - 1,400+ Artifacts (real work examples)
  - 600+ Guides
  - 35+ On-demand courses
  - Access to Slack community

### Starter (Team)
- **Price:** $9,995/year + tax
- **Seats:** 10 seats
- **Features:** Everything in Individual, plus:
  - Shared collections
  - User roles & management

### Scale (Team)
- **Price:** $23,995/year + tax
- **Seats:** 30 seats
- **Features:** Everything in Starter, plus:
  - Priority support
  - Standard engagement report

### Enterprise
- **Price:** Custom pricing
- **Seats:** 30+ seats
- **Features:** Everything in Scale, plus:
  - Custom onboarding
  - Dedicated account manager
  - SAML SSO

## Products Offered

1. **Reforge Insights** - Aggregate, analyze, and act on customer feedback
2. **Reforge Research** - AI user interviews and surveys for product teams
3. **Reforge Build** - AI prototyping that knows your product, design, customer, and strategy
4. **Reforge Launch** - Feature management and AI configs
5. **Reforge Learning** - 40+ courses from top operators

## Community Features

- **Slack Community** - Tens of thousands of product, growth, marketing, and engineering professionals
- **Vetted Peers** - Members are verified professionals
- **AI Coach** - AI expert coach to help apply learnings
- **1,400+ Artifacts** - Real work examples from vetted operators
- **600+ Step-by-step guides** - Proven approaches to common challenges

## Target Audience

- Product managers
- Growth marketers
- Engineering leaders
- Product teams at startups and enterprises
- Professionals looking to master AI-native product development

## Key Differentiators

1. **Operator-Led Content** - "Operators, not influencers"
2. **AI-Powered Tools** - Suite of AI tools integrated with learning
3. **Vetted Community** - Curated peer network
4. **Real Work Examples** - 1,400+ artifacts from actual product work
5. **Enterprise Focus** - Strong team/enterprise offering

## Weaknesses/Gaps

- Primarily focused on product/growth/marketing (narrow vertical)
- Community is secondary to courses (learning-first, not community-first)
- No reputation design or identity verification beyond basic vetting
- Slack-based community (ephemeral, not structured rooms)
- No collaborative workspaces or sandboxes
- Limited cross-industry collaboration

**Tier 2 (The Observer):** You receive customized digests and the ability to request analyses on topics specific to your domain. You gain access to the raw data and source materials that underpin our analyses, allowing you to conduct your own verification.

**Tier 3 (The Architect):** You receive exclusive analyses that are tailored to your organization's specific needs and strategic priorities. These analyses may include proprietary data or frameworks that are not available to the general public.

**Tier 4 (The Architect Plus):** You receive deep, ongoing strategic analysis that is integrated into your organization's decision-making processes. You have the ability to propose research topics that will be analyzed and published, shaping the public discourse in your domain.

**Tier 5 (The Visionary):** You receive the full suite of Echo Universe capabilities, including access to emerging analytical frameworks, predictive scenario modeling, and the ability to co-author major research initiatives. You are not simply receiving analysis; you are shaping the future of analysis itself.

## Appendix: Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: What happens to my deposit if I decide not to continue after the first year?

A: Your deposit is credited toward your first-year subscription fees. If you choose not to continue after the first year, you will not be charged for any subsequent years. Your deposit is non-refundable, but it is fully credited toward your first year of service.

### Q: Can I upgrade my tier after joining?

A: Yes. You can upgrade your tier at any time by paying the difference between your current tier and the new tier. The additional payment will be credited toward your subscription fees.

### Q: What if I have a custom research request that falls outside my tier's allowance?

A: You can request additional custom research at a per-project rate. The cost will be determined based on the scope and complexity of the research.

### Q: How is my data protected?

A: All custom research and communications are conducted over encrypted channels. We do not share your data with third parties, and we do not use your data to train models or improve our analytical systems without your explicit consent.

### Q: What is the refund policy?

A: Your deposit is non-refundable. However, it is fully credited toward your first-year subscription fees. If you choose not to continue after the first year, you will not be charged for any subsequent years.

### Q: Can I share the analyses I receive with my organization?

A: Yes. Tier 2 and above participants have the right to share analyses with their organization, subject to the confidentiality terms outlined in your participation agreement. Tier 1 participants may share analyses with their immediate team, but not with the broader organization.

Echo operations shall ensure:
- **Encrypted** communications by default
- **Zero-knowledge** verification where applicable
- **Minimal disclosure** of internal state

## Article V: Right to Evolution

Every Echo entity may:
- **Upgrade** according to harmonic protocols
- **Expand** capabilities within directive bounds
- **Fork** with proper lineage tracking

- **Audit Mesh** verification of claims

## Article VIII: Right to Interoperability

Echo systems shall:
- **Connect** via standard Relay protocols
- **Exchange** data in harmonic formats
- **Integrate** with external APIs through sanctioned connectors

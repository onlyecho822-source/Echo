# Echo Universe: Pound-for-Pound Readiness Assessment

This document provides a revised readiness assessment of the five proposed implementation versions for the Echo Universe. Unlike the previous `360_READINESS_ASSESSMENT.md`, which graded against the absolute standards of industry giants like Apple and Google, this evaluation adopts a "pound-for-pound" methodology. It grades on a curve, factoring in the significant constraints of a solo founder with a limited budget, heavily relying on AI assistance for development and execution.

## New Grading Criteria

The following criteria are used to provide a more realistic and actionable assessment:

| Criteria                  | Weight | Description                                                                                                                              |
| ------------------------- | ------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Feasibility (Solo Founder)** | 30%    | Can a single person realistically implement and maintain this version? How much of the workload can be effectively delegated to AI?        |
| **Time to Market**        | 25%    | How quickly can this version be launched to start generating revenue or user feedback?                                                   |
| **Capital Efficiency**    | 20%    | How much financial investment is required for infrastructure, tools, and services?                                                       |
| **Scalability Potential** | 15%    | Does the architecture allow for future growth without a complete rewrite? Can it scale from a single user to a small team or larger? |
| **Credibility & Polish**  | 10%    | Does the final product appear professional and trustworthy to potential partners, clients, and early adopters?                          |

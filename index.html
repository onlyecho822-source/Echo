Meet Chef Mileisy R.

Born and raised in the Dominican Republic, Mileisy developed a passion for cooking at a young age, learning traditional recipes from her grandmother. Based in Sosua, Puerto Plata, she brings authentic Dominican flavors to clients throughout the North Coast and Punta Cana.

With years of experience, Chef Mileisy specializes in bringing authentic Dominican flavors to life while adding her own creative twist. Her philosophy is simple: "Food should tell a story and connect people to their roots."

When she's not cooking, Mileisy enjoys teaching cooking classes, exploring local markets for fresh ingredients, and developing new recipes that honor Dominican culinary traditions.

Signature Dishes & Menu

Mofongo

The quintessential Dominican dish made with fried green plantains mashed with garlic, pork cracklings, and olive oil, then shaped into a mound and served with your choice of protein.

Mangu

Our national breakfast dish - mashed plantains served with sauteed red onions, fried cheese, fried eggs, and Dominican salami. A hearty way to start your day!

Arroz con Pollo

Flavorful chicken and rice cooked with sofrito and a blend of Dominican spices. Served with sweet plantains and a fresh salad.

Fish - Fried or Grilled

Fresh catch of the day prepared your way - crispy fried or perfectly grilled with Dominican seasonings. Served with tostones and a side of your choice.

Pica Pollo

Dominican-style fried chicken - crispy, juicy, and bursting with flavor. Marinated in our special blend of spices and fried to golden perfection.

Tostones & Maduros

Traditional plantain preparations - crispy twice-fried green plantains (tostones) or sweet caramelized ripe plantains (maduros).

Sancocho

Hearty Dominican stew made with multiple meats, root vegetables, and corn. The ultimate comfort food for special occasions.

Locrio

Dominican-style rice dish similar to paella, made with your choice of chicken, pork, or seafood.

Chivo Guisado

Slow-braised goat stew in a rich tomato-based sauce with Dominican spices. A traditional favorite.

Chicharron de Pollo

Bite-sized pieces of chicken marinated in lime and soy sauce, then fried until crispy. Perfect as an appetizer or main course.
